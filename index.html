How it works

A game your legs can win

No leaderboard anxiety, no streak-shaming, no ads. Pick a pace that fits your life and let ordinary walking carry you somewhere extraordinary.

01

Pick your pace

Stroller, Wanderer, or Trailblazer — every tier plays the same game, with goals scaled to you. Five good days out of seven makes a week.

02

Just walk

Steps flow in automatically from Apple Health or Health Connect. No check-ins, no logging, no fuss.

03

Travel the route

Your steps move a marker along real journeys. Checkpoints send you postcards; finished routes hang trophies on your wall.

04

Earn real rewards

Weekly goals earn tokens for partner discounts and free gear, services, and experiences. Miss a week? Rewards queue — they're never taken away.

The health-data promise

Your steps are yours

  • Steps are the only health data we read. Read-only, with your explicit permission, revocable any time.
  • We never read your location. Your position on a journey is computed from step counts alone.
  • We never sell your data — and our analytics are structurally blocked from ever containing a step count.
  • Sponsors never see your numbers. Rewards share only your first name and email, and only when you claim one.

The full policy is short and written in plain English: wandelo.app/privacy
